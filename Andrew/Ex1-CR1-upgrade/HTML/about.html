I used to play the violin everyday but now my fingers are tired from coding. Music was a super fun career until I had kids and realised there were more mouths to feed than just my own. A friend of mine told me the world needs more web developers and suggested I find a boot camp. at first I thought it was a crazy idea but then Covid-19 happened and finished off the dying embers of an already dwindling career in which teaching had long since overtaken performing. In French you would say C'est la vie! That means "Such is life" although it literally means "that/it is life" just like you would say in English. Languages are important and interesting to study but sometimes I think they are just imaginary barriers between people who are kind of just the same...
